  • Company Overview

    Lead intern is an initiative to bridge the gap between the students seeking learning opportunities and the corporate world that require talented and hard-working individuals, to help them grown in this competitive world

  • Our Mission

    We make sure that you get worthy internships . We believe in creating awareness of the opportunities present around the globe. Changing the way internships were. Its not just another business , its a brand in making.

  • Why To Choose Us?

    We at LEAD INTERN believe that this culture of education we are following is becoming toxic day by day. Data based learning is increasing and experiential learning is almost negligible. We believe in doing rather than talking. Learn things and implement them with a simple motive "DO WHAT YOU LOVE"

  • Benefits Of Internship

    As an important part of the professional development, internships shape a person for future. Any recruit with work experience is preferred and in today’s cut-throat competition an internship can achieve you that split second win. Gain as an intern; experience, skills, build character and perhaps reap a few dollars.
